## 🏢 Platform Overview

### Viator (TripAdvisor Company)

**Founded:** 1995  
**Inventory:** 300,000+ experiences  
**Partners:** 20,000+ tour operators  
**Market Position:** Industry veteran, largest inventory  
**Parent Company:** TripAdvisor (acquired 2014)

**Key Strengths:**
- Massive inventory (300K+ tours)
- TripAdvisor integration (verified reviews)
- 24-hour cancellation policy (90% of inventory)
- Established trust and brand recognition
- Price match guarantee
- 24/7 customer service
- Mobile app with offline capabilities

**Key Weaknesses:**
- Overwhelming choice (decision fatigue)
- Generic user experience
- Commission-heavy model (20-30%)
- Less focus on unique/curated experiences
- No AI-powered recommendations
- Complex search and filtering

**Founded:** 2009

**Market Position:** Innovation leader, curated experiences

- Modern, intuitive UI/UX
- Focus on sustainable tourism
- Smaller group sizes
- Off-the-beaten-path activities
- Mobile-first design
- Flexible cancellation
- Exclusive partnerships
